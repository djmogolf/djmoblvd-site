Before you ask

The questions couples ask us most — answered. Still wondering about something? Send your date over and we'll take care of the rest.

Do you take song requests?+

Every package includes a customized personal playlist. We build your must-play and do-not-play lists together in unlimited consultations — and Mo reads the room from there.

Will you MC our announcements?+

Yes — professional emcee services come standard in every package. Grand entrance, toasts, first dance, send-off: every moment is announced and on time.

Is a Retainer required to secure a booking?+

Yes, a retainer is necessary to confirm your booking. The exact amount and payment schedule will be discussed during the booking process.

What happens if our event extends beyond the booked time?+

Flexibility is key! If your celebration extends, additional time can often be arranged on the day itself. We'll discuss options during the planning process and ensure everything runs smoothly.

Do you travel outside Las Vegas?+

Based in Las Vegas, available anywhere. DJ Mo has played destination events from Miami Beach to Northern California.

What is "Beyond The Aisle"?+

A content-creation add-on included in The Lakes package: They specialize in telling unforgettable wedding stories through short form video and photo content all captured on an iPhone.

How far in advance should I book?+

As early as possible to secure your date — popular dates fill up quickly. We advise booking at least 6–12 months in advance.

Can I customize elements within the packages?+

Absolutely. The packages are designed as a foundation, and we welcome customization to tailor the experience — mix in any à la carte add-on.
