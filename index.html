07

Before you ask

Do you take song requests?+

Every package includes a customized personal playlist. We build your must-play and do-not-play lists together in unlimited consultations — and Mo reads the room from there.

Will you MC our announcements?+

Yes — professional emcee services come standard in every package. Grand entrance, toasts, first dance, send-off: every moment is announced and on time.

Can you cover our ceremony too?+

The Anthem package and up include on-site ceremony music plus wireless microphones for your officiant and your vows — so every word is heard in the back row.

What does pricing look like?+

Three packages — Anthem from $2,000, Paradise from $2,500, and The Lakes from $4,000 — plus à la carte add-ons like photo booth, cold sparklers, and dancing on the clouds. See the full packages & pricing page.

Do you travel outside Las Vegas?+

Based in Las Vegas, available anywhere. DJ Mo has played destination events from Miami Beach to Northern California.

What is “Beyond The Aisle”?+

A content-creation add-on included in The Lakes package: candid, social-ready video captured at your event — your highlights, ready for Instagram and TikTok.
